Dataset: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rohullahakbari12/weather-prediction

**ОПИСАНИЕ ДАТАСЕТА:**
 
1.   Date: The date of the weather observation.
2.   MinTemp: The minimum temperature recorded on that day.
3.   MaxTemp: The maximum temperature recorded on that day.
4.   Rainfall: The amount of rainfall measured in millimeters.
5.   Evaporation: The amount of water evaporation measured in millimeters.
6.   Sunshine: The duration of sunshine in hours.
7.   WindGustDir: The direction of the strongest wind gust.
8.   WindGustSpeed: The speed of the strongest wind gust.
9.   WindDir9am: The wind direction at 9 am.
10.   WindDir3pm: The wind direction at 3 pm.
11.   WindSpeed9am: The wind speed at 9 am.
12.   WindSpeed3pm: The wind speed at 3 pm.
13.   Humidity9am: The humidity level at 9 am.
14.   Humidity3pm: The humidity level at 3 pm.
15.   Pressure9am: The atmospheric pressure at 9 am.
16.   Pressure3pm: The atmospheric pressure at 3 pm.
17.   Cloud9am: The fraction of sky covered by clouds at 9 am.
18.   Cloud3pm: The fraction of sky covered by clouds at 3 pm.
19.   Temp9am: The temperature at 9 am.
20.   Temp3pm: The temperature at 3 pm.
21.   RainToday: Whether there was rain recorded on that day (Yes/No).
22.   RainTomorrow: The target variable indicating whether it rained the following day (Yes/No).
